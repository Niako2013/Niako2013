dog helth

Dog health depends on proper nutrition, regular exercise, vaccinations, parasite control, dental care, and routine vet checkups. Watch for signs of illness like vomiting, lethargy, loss of appetite, or behavioral changes. Keep their weight in check and provide mental stimulation. Regular grooming and a safe environment also contribute to overall well-being.
